About Us

Discover our restaurant story

Located in the vibrant heart of Danapur Bazar, The Woods is a beloved culinary destination where tradition meets innovation. Renowned for its commitment to premium ingredients, rich flavors, and exceptional service, The Woods offers a dining experience like no other. Step inside and you’ll find an elegantly curated space — warm lighting, rustic charm, and tasteful decor blend to create a cozy yet refined ambiance. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or gathering for a leisurely meal, The Woods promises comfort, flavor, and memories in every visit.

Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

The login hours for the restaurant’s portal or app are from 9:00 AM to 11:30 PM.

What do i get my refund?

To request a refund, please contact our support team within 24 hours of your order. Refunds are processed based on the issue reported, and typically take 3–5 business days to reflect in your account.

How long it will take food to arrive?

Delivery times range from 30 to 60 minutes, depending on your location and order volume. You’ll receive real-time updates once your order is placed.

Does your restaurant has both veg and non veg?

Yes, we serve a wide variety of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. Our menu is curated to cater to diverse tastes and dietary preferences.

What is cost of food delivery?

Delivery charges may vary based on distance, but typically range between ₹30 to ₹80. Free delivery is available for orders above ₹500 within 5 km.

Who is eligible for pro membership?

Anyone who signs up through our app or website can become a Pro Member. Membership includes benefits like exclusive discounts, free delivery on select orders, and early access to special menus.
